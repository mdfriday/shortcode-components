# MasterPoster001 Component

The MasterPoster001 component displays a stylish poster featuring a fishing master with their fishing tips and advice.

    masterName="天元邓刚" 
    answerTitle="锦囊： " 
    answer="钓流水，钓离底" 
    detailText="中午温度升高，缺氧，鱼起浮，流水含氧量高，且食物充足。"
